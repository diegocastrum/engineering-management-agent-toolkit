# Skill Examples

Use these prompts to sanity-check trigger behavior after installing the skills.

## `first-week-snapshot`

Should trigger:

- "I just got promoted to manage the team I was on. What do I do in week 1?"
- "I'm inheriting a team whose previous manager left last Friday. Where do I start?"
- "I joined a new company yesterday as an EM. Help me structure my first week."
- "End of week 1 — help me synthesize what I've heard into the snapshot."

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me write a performance review."
- "Design the interview loop for a staff engineer."

## `manager-toolkit`

Should trigger:

- "I'm drowning in email and forgetting things. How do I get organized?"
- "Where does my time actually go? Help me audit my calendar."
- "I can't get any deep work done — my calendar is a mess."
- "What does Grove's output equation actually look like for an engineering manager?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me prep my 1-1 with Sarah tomorrow."
- "We have a launch next week and we're behind. What do I cut?"

## `one-to-ones-stanier`

Should trigger:

- "I have my first 1-1 with a new direct report tomorrow. Help me run a contracting session."
- "My 1-1s have turned into status meetings. How do I reset them?"
- "Here are my notes from today's 1-1 — help me extract actions."
- "They keep cancelling 1-1s. How do I get the cadence back?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Write the performance review for Sarah."
- "Should we put Sarah on a PIP?"

## `motivation-and-fit`

Should trigger:

- "One of my reports seems bored — they used to be a star. What's going on?"
- "How do I figure out what motivates this senior engineer?"
- "I have a stretch project — who on the team would it land well with?"
- "They want a challenge but I don't have any new projects to give them."

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me write Sarah's perf review."
- "I think someone is about to quit."

## `performance-reviews-stanier`

Should trigger:

- "Review season is coming up. Help me set up the tracker."
- "Draft a peer feedback request email for Sarah's review."
- "I need to write Sarah's review — here's the form. Help me fill it out."
- "We're coupling pay to reviews this cycle and I think it's a bad idea."

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me prep my 1-1 with Sarah tomorrow."
- "Design the interview loop for a senior backend engineer."

## `hiring-stanier`

Should trigger:

- "Help me write a job description for a senior backend engineer."
- "Design the interview funnel for this role — what stages and what they cover."
- "Audit this JD for gendered language."
- "We're about to give a take-home — what are the rules?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me build a growth plan for an existing engineer."
- "Write the performance review for Sarah."

## `stress-and-pressure`

Should trigger:

- "We have a launch next week and we're behind. What do we cut?"
- "Leadership wants us to throw more engineers at this. Is that a good idea?"
- "MoSCoW the backlog for me — we have three weeks to ship."
- "I feel like a fraud running this team. Help me think through it."

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me write a JD."
- "I want to talk to one of my reports about their career."

## `information-and-politics`

Should trigger:

- "I just learned the team is being reorganized in two weeks. Can I tell them?"
- "Another manager told me one of their reports is on a PIP — what do I do with that?"
- "I disagree with the decision leadership made but they want me to back it. How do I do that?"
- "How do I push back on a peer without escalating to my manager?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me prep my 1-1 with Sarah."
- "Draft this performance review."

## `letting-go-and-self-care`

Should trigger:

- "I can't stop checking email at night. Help me wind down."
- "I delegated this and I keep wanting to take it back. How do I let go?"
- "I'm working evenings and weekends and I can't sustain it."
- "I'm anxious about something I can't actually control. How do I let it go?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Help me design the interview loop."
- "Help me write a review."

## `team-housekeeping`

Should trigger:

- "We had an incident last week. Help me run a five whys."
- "How do I start a guild for the backend engineers across teams?"
- "We need to start writing ADRs — how do I introduce it?"
- "Run a team health check with me — what dimensions should we score?"

Should not be the primary skill:

- "I need to give a piece of corrective feedback to one engineer."
- "Help me prep my 1-1 with Sarah."

## `career-vision-exercise`

Should trigger:

- "Sarah is a senior engineer and we want to talk about her 10-year arc, not just the next promo."
- "Help me deliver the career vision exercise as a series of coaching sessions to my report."
- "I want to do this exercise for myself — where am I going next?"
- "Help me extract a skills backlog from this career vision."

Should not be the primary skill:

- "Sarah's promo packet — help me write it."
- "We need to plan this quarter's deliverables."
